🏰 Impossible Tower Defense

🎯 Goal

Stop enemies from reaching the red exit row at the bottom. If too many leak through, you lose!

🔨 Building

Tap Build Mode, then tap empty cells to place bunkers ($50 each). Bunkers block enemies and create a maze — but you can't fully block the path.

👥 Units

Tap a bunker to open it. Add units inside to attack enemies:

  • MG — Fast, steady damage
  • Shotgun — Splash damage, short range
  • Flamethrower — AOE + burn DOT
  • Sniper — Huge damage, long range, slow

Units can be upgraded to Tier 5 for more damage, range, and fire rate.

👾 Enemies

  • Grunt — Standard
  • Runner — Fast, fragile
  • Tank — Slow, massive HP
  • Swarm — Tiny, many
  • Boss — Every 10 waves. Good luck.
