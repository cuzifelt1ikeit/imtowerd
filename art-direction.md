# Art Direction вҖ” Impossible Tower Defense

## Sprite Sizes
- **Base tile**: 16Г—16 (GBA-style pixel art)
- **Grid unit**: 8px (everything snaps to multiples)
- Swarm enemies: 8Г—8
- Standard enemies (grunt/runner): 16Г—16
- Tank enemies: 16Г—16 or 24Г—24
- Boss enemies: 32Г—32
- Bunker: 16Г—16 base structure
- Bunker units: Not full sprites вҖ” just colored indicator dots (units aren't visible enough to warrant detailed art)

## Style
- GBA-era pixel art, crunchy and readable
- Multiple civilization themes for enemy waves
- Each civ maps to 5 enemy types: Grunt, Runner, Tank, Swarm, Boss
